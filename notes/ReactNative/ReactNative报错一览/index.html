📱1 配置问题

1.1 Cannot find font family FontAwesome

react-native-icon/FontAwesome妹配置好。要配置的地方有：

  1. ios/[Program Name]/info.plist文件
  2. Xcode中依据github给出的提示配置。我这里import后已自动配好。

💻 2 真机调试 IOS

2.1 Xcode : Errors were encountered while preparing your device for development

尝试force reboot后再次连接电脑，遇到[Device] is busy: Copying cache files from device。****重新连接后等待2min就好了。看网上说重启Xcode也可以。

2.2 finished with error [-1001] Error

显示的是网络连接和端口的问题。重启Xcode后解决。
